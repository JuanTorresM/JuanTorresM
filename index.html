- Define a problem that you want to explore:

People not waking up during the morning because they set their alarm on PM not on AM.

- Introduction to the problem (5w +1: What, Who, When, Where, Why, How)

- What: People set their alarm on PM not on AM and when the alarm goes off they don’t wake up completely which leads to stay sleeping

- Who: Anyone that needs to wake up during the morning mainly students and workers

- When: Late at night probably during midnight and during the morning

- Where: Their house or anywhere in which people need their alarm

- Why: We as humans make mistakes very often and one of the mistakes we make is setting the alarm at the wrong time.

- How: Not paying attention when they set the alarm
